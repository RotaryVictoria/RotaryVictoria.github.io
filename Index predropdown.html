Re-Thinking How We Do Rotary

This club is designed to be active, flexible, accessible and economical for leaders and professionals in Victoria. We have created this club so Rotary can fit into your life not the other way around.

Active

The RCDV is dedicated to community involvement and meeting like minded individuals who share the same values. Help us make a difference locally and internationally, attend a meeting and share your ideas!

Accessible

Club membership is open to all people of all age whether you are employed, care for your family or are looking for work. In order to be as accesible to the most amount of people, RCDV meets at night time, so you can come along in the evening and maybe have a drink or a bite to eat, that’s completely up to you. Don't think you can be a Rotarian? Think again!

Economical

Rotary shouldn’t cost a fortune. RCDV intends to keep costs low, so we can focus on the important aspects of being a Rotarian. There is no meal requirement, it is at your discretion if you purchase food or drink.

Flexible

We understand that people lead busy lives and it can be daunting to figure out how you will fit Rotary into it! Don't fear, membership of this club is flexible. You can meet your two event minmium through weekly meetings, networking/Professional Development opportunities, social and volunteering events.Can't make a meeting, or live far away? You can skype in!

Why Join Rotary?

Rotary is the whole package, there are so many opportunities intertwined in one organisation. Here are the three main reasons why everyone should be part of Rotary

Rotary Advantage

Being a Rotarian makes you stand out, it gives you opportunities that just wouldn’t be available to you without Rotary. It allows you the opportunity to network with a variety of professionalism town. It also connects you to professionals throughout the world. Rotary has over 1.3 million members all over the world. Rotarians network on an international level.

Rotary Leadership

Rotary also provides plenty of opportunities for you to develop as a leader and professional. Conferences, workshops and leadership opportunities are provided to Rotarians to help us become better leaders in our community and in our profession.

Rotary Service

Rotary also provides you with the opportunity to give back to your local community and communities worldwide. We work on creating volunteer and fundraising opportunities for members to take part in. We welcome members ideas for events so if you are passionate about a particular cause Rotary provide the forum for you to help.

What is Rotary?

You may have seen the Rotary symbol around your town or heard a little about what Rotary does from friends or family. But do you really know what Rotary International is?

Facts

  • Started in 1905 by Paul Harris so a group of friends meeting to connect professionally and to work together to make a difference in their community
  • Rotary is the world's first service organisation with 1.3 million members worldwide
  • Rotary has clubs in over 200 countries across the world, allowing you to network on a global scale

Goals

  • Connect leaders from all continents, cultures and occupations
  • Develop leadership skills whether you are a student, starting your career or already established, Rotary provides you with the tools to become a better leader
  • Exchnge ideas by expanding your social network and your worldview. Rotary allows you to share diverse perspective from all other the world
  • Most importantly, Rotary enables you to take action and make a lasting change in communities around the world

Achievements

  • Rotary supports the educational aspirations of our future leaders through numerous scholarships and Rotary's Peace Fellowships
  • Rotary supports and develops the youth of the world through it's youth centered programs: Interact, Rotaract and Youth Exchange
  • Rotary's key iniative is End Polio Now. Since 1988 there were 125 Polio endemic countries now there are only 3 Polio endemic countries. This change is due Rotarians all over the world
  • All other the world, there are service projects happening, organised and lead by Rotarians at a grass roots level wanting to make a difference. This is the power of Rotary
