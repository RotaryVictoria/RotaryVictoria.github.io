Introducing 1000x5

Thank you for your support. Just by buying a ticket for the swing dance will help us ensure that 4000+ local babies and toddlers are regularly receving books.

We Collect Books

Families donate gently used books for babies and preschoolers at local elementary schools. Our partners also donate books or provide financial assistance so that we can purchase books.

We Sort Books

Our volunteers meet weekly to clean and sort the donated books and put them into gift bags. We use gift bags to convey the value of the books. By presenting books in an appealing package we convey that reading is a special activity and that books are treasured.

We Partner With Our Community

The gift bags are then distributed to Strong Start Centres and various agencies that work with families. These organizations get the books into the homes of families with small children.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I pay with cash or cheque?

We're happy to accept cash or cheque. Just head to our online reservation form to reserve your tickets and select pay cash or cheque on the night.

Was my payment processed? The Stripe box where I inputted my credit disappeared.

If your payment is a success, you'll see a quick green checkmark and then the payment box will disappear. If your card is declined, then the credit card box will shake and turn red, indicating a problem.
So yes, if the box disappeared you're all good.

Can I get a receipt? Is it tax-deductible?

One will be mailed to you. And unfortunately, donations to the club do not count as tax-deductible at this time. We're looking into it!

My card was declined for the payment, when I know it shouldn't be. What's up?

Please let us know if this happens to you, because we've only seen it happen once. We suspect it's an issue of having two credit card transactions in a short time being automatically blocked for fraud protection. Either way, let us know and we'll find a solution.

I'm still lost. Can I talk to a person?

Of course! Just send us an email at rotarydowntownvictoria@gmail.com.
