Polio Pub Crawl

Eradicating Polio One Shot At A Time

These are the rules and guidelines for RCDV's Polio Pub Crawl. We've kept this short and sweet, so you can get straight to it. Please read them once, otherwise you might get confused and wake up tomorrow with a splitting headache on a fishing ship to Port Alberni.

Overview

We have a list of tasks and places in Downtown Victoria, and your mission is to accomplish and visit as many as you can before 10:30pm. That's when we'll all meet up at Sticky Wicket 10:30pm, so Sam can pick the winner. Don't be late!

To prove you've been to every nook and cranny in Victoria you'll have to take photos. Upload your photos to Twitter, Facebook, or Instagram with #rotarypubcrawl and you're good to go. Alternatively, if you're not on social media, you can just show the photos at Sticky.

Rules

  • Once you're downtown, you can only get around by foot
  • You only get points if you have photos or proof.
  • Uploading to social media with #rotarypubcrawl counts as submitting, but you can also show them in person.
  • One point for each task
  • Be at Sticky Wicket for 10:30pm, we are on a guest list so no need to queue. Find Sam who will be waiting around the entrance once you are inside.
  1. Upload at least 3 photos to instagram/twitter with #rotarypubcrawl
  2. Take a photo with someone wearing a costume
  3. Play drink roulette at Veneto (1450 Douglas)
  4. Get a stranger to buy a team member a drink
  5. Get a hug from a stranger
  6. Do a random act of kindness
  7. Enjoy a double seafood Caesar at Barts (777 Douglas)
  8. Have a beer of what is on tap at The Drake (777 Douglas)
  9. Have a belini at the Cactus Club Cafe (1125 Douglas)
  10. Enjoy a shot of Jamesons at the Irish Times (1200 Government St)
  11. Have a flight of beers at Swans (506 Pandora Ave)
  12. Have a Tiki cocktail at Clives (740 Burdett Ave)
  13. Get a picture with a spirit whale
  14. Enjoy a drink of your choice at the Bard and Banker (1022 Government St)
  15. Enjoy some international beer at Canoe (450 Swift St)
  16. Get behind the bar and make a drink
  17. Eat some nuts at Big Bad Johns
  18. Drink a pint of Hoyne beer at the Garricks Head (66 Bastion Square)
  19. Show your Victoria pride, get a group shot outside the Empress and upload to Instagram/Twitter #rotarypubcrawl
  20. Visit three venues of your choice
